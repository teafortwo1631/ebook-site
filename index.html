From Frustration to Conversation

How AI Chatbots Are Changing Customer Service — a calm, human-centered introduction to AI communication designed to feel familiar and approachable.

About the Book

Many people feel overwhelmed by technology, especially when communication becomes automated. This e-book explores how AI chatbots can support real conversations without losing the human touch.

About the Author

Tricia D. Hamill focuses on communication, empathy, and the balance between traditional human interaction and emerging AI tools.
